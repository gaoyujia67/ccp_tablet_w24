Cream Stew

Cream stew, a comforting Japanese dish, is a velvety blend of vegetables, meat, and a luscious white sauce. This soul-soothing creation combines the heartiness of a stew with the silkiness of cream, resulting in a rich and flavorful culinary experience that warms both the body and the spirit.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • onions
  • potatoes
  • carrots
  • broccoli
  • mushrooms
  • light cream or milk
  • flour
  • salt
  • chopped parsley
  • pepper

Cooking Procedures

  1. Fry meat and onions in hot oil
  2. Add vegetables and sprinkle with flour and mix well
  3. Add light cream or milk or both, add salt and black pepper
  4. Wait for the food to cook and the milk to collect juices
  5. Sprinkle chopped parsley and black pepper

Spare Ribs Soup

Spare Ribs Soup is a savory delight that tantalizes the taste buds with its tender meat and flavorful broth. Boasting succulent spare ribs, vegetables, and aromatic spices, this hearty soup is a culinary journey that combines nourishment and indulgence, offering a comforting and satisfying experience with every spoonful.

Ingredients

  • Pork Ribs
  • Green Onions
  • Sweet Corn
  • Soup Dumplings
  • Ginger
  • Cooking Wine
  • Star Anise
  • Fragrant leaves

Cooking Procedures

  1. Soak the pork ribs in water and flour for 15 minutes and wash
  2. Add water, green onions, ginger, cooking wine, two star anise, and three fragrant leaves to the pork ribs
  3. Bring to a boil over high heat and cook for two minutes. Skim off the foam
  4. Discard water and spices
  5. Lightly fry the pork ribs in peanut oil
  6. Pour in water, add four-material soup dumplings (Poria, Gorgon seeds, lotus seeds, yam), add sweet corn or sweet carrots, yam or white radish or winter melon, and red dates
  7. Bring to a boil over high heat, then simmer over low heat for more than 1 hour
  8. After cooking, add salt, green onions, and black pepper

Kong Pao Chicken

Kung Pao Chicken, a classic Sichuan dish in China, is a flavorful stir-fry featuring tender chicken, crunchy peanuts, and vibrant vegetables in a spicy, tangy sauce.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Peanuts
  • Bell peppers
  • Scallions
  • Garlic, ginger
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Chili flakes

Cooking Procedures

  1. Marinate chicken in soy sauce and set aside.
  2. Stir-fry chicken until golden.
  3. Add garlic, ginger, and vegetables.
  4. Mix in soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, and chili flakes.
  5. Toss in peanuts and scallions.
  6. Serve over rice and enjoy this aromatic delight!

Brownie Cake

Brownie cakes, a delectable fusion of rich chocolate and indulgent cake, are a delightful treat for any sweet tooth.

Ingredients

  • Unsalted butter
  • Granulated sugar
  • Large eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • All-purpose flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Chocolate chips, hunks

Baking Procedures

  1. Preheat your oven to 350째F (175째C) and grease a baking pan.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter. Allow it to cool slightly.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  4. Add the melted butter to the sugar mixture and mix thoroughly.
  5. In another bowl, sift together flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  7. Fold in the chocolate chips or chunks, distributing them evenly throughout the batter.
  8. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for approximately 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs (not wet batter).
  10. Allow the brownie cake to cool in the pan before cutting it into squares or slices.

Apple Pie

Apple pie holds a cherished place in American culinary history, symbolizing tradition and comfort. Its roots trace back to early European settlers, who brought apple seeds and pie-making skills to the New World. By the 19th century, apple pie became synonymous with American identity, reinforced by the famous phrase "as American as apple pie." This iconic dessert often graces Thanksgiving and Fourth of July tables, fostering a sense of familial warmth and nostalgia.

Ingredents

  • Apple slices
  • Granulated sugar
  • All-purpose flour
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Ground nutmeg
  • Lemon juice
  • Refrigerated pie crusts

Baking Procedures

  1. Preheat your oven to 425째F (220째C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine sliced apples, sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice. Toss until apples are evenly coated.
  3. Roll out one pie crust and place it in a 9-inch pie dish.
  4. Pour the apple mixture into the crust, spreading it evenly.
  5. Roll out the second pie crust and place it over the apple filling. Trim and crimp the edges to seal the pie.
  6. Cut a few slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.
  7. Optional: Brush the top crust with milk and sprinkle with sugar for a golden finish.
  8. Place the pie on a baking sheet to catch any drips and bake in the preheated oven for 45-55 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown, and the filling is bubbly.
  9. Allow the apple pie to cool before serving. Enjoy it on its own or with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a classic treat that never goes out of style.
